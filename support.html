FAQ

How do I adjust tempo?

Tap the "+" or "-" buttons on the home screen to increase or decrease the tempo.

No sound?

Make sure your device is not muted or on silent mode. Also check that the app's volume is turned up.

How do I change the time signature?

Look for the time signature selector in the app settings or main interface.

Can I use custom sounds?

The app includes several built-in metronome sounds.

Troubleshooting

If you're experiencing issues:

  • Try restarting the app
  • Make sure you have the latest version installed
  • Check your device's audio settings
  • Contact us if the problem persists
